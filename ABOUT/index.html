ABOUT

No Fear. No Limits. Just Fun. Monsters!

Monsquad expresses the free-spirited and rebellious nature of teenagers in street, skateboarding, and subculture in various ways by comparing them to villains. Monsquad was created in 2022 and the name is a combination of ‘monster,’ referring to supervillain monsters fanatical about skating, and “squad,” representing team spirit. Monsquad is a skateboarding brand based on an all-round skateboarding style, ready to attract the attention of teenagers.

LOGO

The slightly tilted M represents the shape of the skateboard
soaring into the sky. And the awesome potential of teenagers is symbolized by the monster squad’s uneven arrangement and form. These combine to inspire a sense of dynamism and reflects the vitality and individuality of teenagers.
